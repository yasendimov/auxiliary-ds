## Mission

### Mission Statement

**Build swarms, not drones.**

We transform individual drones into AI-enabled nodes of a larger network — creating resilient, coordinated swarms across air, land, and sea. Our open architecture gives democracies scalable, interoperable autonomy that resists jamming and wins at mass scale.

### Vision

**The leading platform for autonomous systems.**

We envision millions of unmanned vehicles connected by one common operating system — swarming as a decisive force across defense, industrial, and civil domains, uniting allies and manufacturers into a shared ecosystem for autonomy.

### Positioning

Auterion occupies a unique position at the intersection of interoperable architecture, AI-driven autonomy, and defense-grade reliability. Unlike proprietary systems that lock customers into single-vendor drones, Auterion provides the operating system that knits fleets together.

### Elevator Pitch

Auterion delivers the operating system for swarms. AuterionOS provides an open architecture that integrates fleets from multiple manufacturers into a single, interoperable fabric. Skynode avionics add AI-enabled autonomy, secure communications and edge computing so that one operator can control many drones. Our battle-proven technology lets vehicles lock onto targets and complete missions even when GPS and video links are jammed. We give democracies an affordable platform for autonomous mass while ensuring that humans remain in control.

### Taglines

**Build Swarms, Not Drones.** — Directly conveys the shift from individual vehicles to coordinated swarms.

**Platform for Autonomous Mass.** — Highlights the focus on large-scale, interoperable autonomy.

**Open Architecture. Swarm Power.** — Combines open systems with swarm capability and democracy.

### Core Values

**Autonomy, AI & Swarm Scale** — Edge computing and AI-driven autonomy at the core. Products like Skynode S allow drones to track moving targets autonomously and complete missions when communications are jammed — scaling from a handful to hundreds.

**Interoperability & Open Architecture** — AuterionOS allows customers to combine different drones and manufacturers into a single architecture, enabling swarming where a single operator controls many drones.

**Democratic Responsibility** — A moral obligation to support liberal democracies. Auterion does not sell to authoritarian regimes. Human operators always remain in control.

**Open-Source Heritage** — Roots in open-source development. Making PX4 freely available created the world's largest developer community for autonomous robots.

**Commercial Affordability & Scale** — Advanced capabilities should be accessible. Skynode S costs roughly the price of an Android phone. Autonomous mass, produced at scale.
